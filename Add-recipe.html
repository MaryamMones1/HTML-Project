Spaghetti

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti noodles
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Cooking Instructions:

1. Boil the spaghetti noodles in salted water according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add chopped onions and sauté until translucent.
3. Add minced garlic and stir for 1 minute.
4. Pour in the canned tomatoes and season with salt and pepper. Let it simmer for 10–15 minutes.
5. Mix the cooked spaghetti with the sauce. Serve hot and garnish with fresh basil.


Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients:

  • 500g boneless chicken
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp grated ginger
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Cooking Instructions:

1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, lemon juice, and all spices. Add chicken pieces and marinate for at least 1 hour.

2. In a large pan, heat oil and sauté onions until soft. Add garlic and ginger and cook for 2 minutes.

3. Add marinated chicken and cook until browned.

4. Stir in crushed tomatoes and simmer for 15 minutes.

5. Add cream, reduce heat, and cook for 10 minutes until sauce thickens.

6. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with naan or rice.


Vegetable Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cold)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 cup chopped carrots
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1/2 cup chopped bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup chopped green beans
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Spring onions for garnish

Cooking Instructions:

1. Heat oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.

2. Add garlic and sauté for a few seconds until fragrant.

3. Add all chopped vegetables and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until slightly tender but still crisp.

4. Add cold cooked rice and stir well to combine with vegetables.

5. Add soy sauce, vinegar, salt, and pepper. Mix everything evenly.

6. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, garnish with chopped spring onions, and serve hot.


Fluffy Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 and 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 and 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp white sugar
  • 1 and 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp melted butter

Cooking Instructions:

1. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.

2. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg, and melted butter; mix until smooth.

3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat.

4. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.

5. Brown on both sides and serve hot with syrup or fruit toppings.
