Menemen

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 green peppers, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, peeled and diced
  • 3 eggs
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Cooking Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan and saut√© the chopped onions and green peppers until soft.
  2. Add the diced tomatoes and cook until they break down and become saucy.
  3. Crack the eggs over the mixture and stir gently.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Cook until the eggs are just set but still soft.
  6. Serve hot with fresh bread.
